Challenges Consequences
Inconsistency: Different teams have different opinions on technology practices. When teams have different practices, they are prone to achieve different outcomes that require multiple resolutions before a full production release.
Platform diversity: Provisioning workflows can vary depending on the target Cloud platforms, VMware or Kubernetes environments. Separate workflow processes across multiple target environments introduce significant overhead for development, release and maintenance.
Image sprawl: Image diversity across multiple platforms leads to an extensive collection of assets. Teams that do not track their image assets centrally contribute to inconsistency, higher costs and security exposures.
