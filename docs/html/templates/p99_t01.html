Approach Benefits
Establish standardization: Use standardized deployment templates to resolve the practices disparities. Teams are neither ahead nor behind in technology choices. And, an intentional synchronization saves time with testing cycles and minimizes edge cases that may undermine the overall health of the final deliverable.
Process alignment: Establish the same process to develop, secure, validate and implement machine images across all Cloud platforms, VMware and Kubernetes environments. Teams meet the same standards for infrastructure creation in development, release and maintenance stages. Teams follow guidelines for human interaction, data format, network protocols, ports and secure logical paths for communication.
Strong Governance: With corporate guidance, teams can automate the promotion of infrastructure to support emerging standards. Teams can plan for emerging improvements to their machine images and promote them using modern DevOps automation patterns from development to pre-production to production stages.
